# Getting Started with WoofTalk

Welcome to WoofTalk! This guide will help you set up the app and make your first translation.

## Step 1: Download the App

- **iOS**: Download from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/wooftalk)
- **Android**: Download from [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.wooftalk.app)
- **Web**: Visit [https://wooftalk.app](https://wooftalk.app) (no download needed!)

## Step 2: Create an Account

1. Open the app
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and password (or use Google/Apple sign-in)
4. Verify your email if required
5. You're in!

## Step 3: Make Your First Translation

1. Tap the **Translate** tab
2. Select source language (Human, Dog, Cat, or Bird)
3. Select target language
4. Type or speak your message
5. Tap **Translate**
6. Hear the result! (and see the text translation)

## Step 4: Explore Premium Features

WoofTalk offers a 3-day free trial of Premium:
- Unlimited translations
- Voice input/output
- Cross-platform sync
- Community phrase access
- Ad-free experience

Tap **Settings** → **Subscription** to start your free trial.

## Step 5: Join the Community

1. Tap **Community** tab
2. Browse user-submitted phrases
3. Upvote your favorites
4. Submit your own translations!
5. Follow other users
