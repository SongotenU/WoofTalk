# WoofTalk FAQs

## General Questions

### What is WoofTalk?
WoofTalk is an app that translates between human and animal languages (Dog, Cat, Bird) using AI. It works on iOS, Android, and Web.

### Is WoofTalk free?
WoofTalk offers a free tier with limited translations. Premium unlocks unlimited translations, voice I/O, and cross-platform sync for $X.99/month (3-day free trial included).

### How accurate are the translations?
Translations are for entertainment purposes. While we use AI models trained on animal vocalizations, results should not be used for animal care decisions. Consult a veterinarian for actual animal communication needs.

## Account & Subscription

### How do I cancel my subscription?
- **iOS**: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → WoofTalk → Cancel
- **Android**: Play Store → Subscriptions → WoofTalk → Cancel
- **Web**: Settings → Subscription → Cancel (Stripe)

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple/Google per their policies. We cannot issue refunds directly. Contact their support for refund requests.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Delete Account**. This permanently removes your data (translations, phrases, profile).

## Translation

### Why is translation slow?
Check your internet connection. Translations require API calls to our AI engine. Slow connections will result in delays.

### Why can't I translate more than X times?
Free tier has daily limits. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited translations.

### Can I translate offline?
Translation requires an internet connection. However, your translation history is cached for offline viewing.

## Community

### How do I submit a phrase?
Go to **Community** → Tap **+** button → Enter translation → Submit. It will be reviewed by the community (voting).

### Can I edit/delete my phrases?
Yes! Go to **Community** → Your Profile → Tap your phrase → Edit or Delete.

### What makes a "good" phrase?
- Accurate translation between human and animal
- Appropriate language (no profanity)
- Original (not copied from others)

## Technical Issues

### App crashes on launch
1. Force close and reopen
2. Restart your device
3. Update to latest app version
4. Reinstall if issue persists

### Translation not working
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify you're signed in
3. Check if you've hit your daily limit (free tier)
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Sync not working across devices
1. Ensure you're signed in with the same account on all devices
2. Check internet connection
3. Force close and reopen the app
4. Check sync status in Settings
