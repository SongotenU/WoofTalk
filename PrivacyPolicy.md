# WoofTalk Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 14, 2025

WoofTalk ("us", "we", or "our") operates the WoofTalk mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Audio Recordings
When you use the translation feature, we collect and process audio recordings of:
- Your voice when speaking to your dog (human-to-dog translation)
- Your dog's vocalizations when listening (dog-to-human translation)

**Purpose:** Audio data is processed locally on your device using on-device machine learning models to generate translations. Audio is not stored or transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable Cloud Sync.

### 1.2 Usage Analytics
We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:
- Translation frequency and duration
- Offline vs online usage patterns
- Feature interactions (button taps, screen views)
- App performance metrics (latency, accuracy)
- Crash reports and error logs

**Purpose:** To enhance app quality, fix bugs, and understand user needs.

### 1.3 Account Information
If you create an account:
- Email address (for account management and support)
- Subscription status and payment information (handled by Apple App Store)
- Translation history (if Cloud Sync enabled, stored encrypted)

**Purpose:** To provide subscription services and optional cloud backup.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Local Processing
All audio translations are performed **locally** on your device:
- No audio is sent to external servers for translation
- Voice data remains private and under your control
- Offline mode works completely without internet

### 2.2 Cloud Features (Opt-in)
If you enable Cloud Sync:
- Translation history is encrypted before upload
- Analytics data is aggregated anonymously
- You can delete your cloud data at any time

### 2.3 Communications
We may send email notifications regarding:
- Subscription status and renewals
- Important app updates or policy changes
- Customer support responses

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell, trade, or rent** your personal information to others.

### 3.1 Service Providers
We use third-party services for app functionality:
- **Apple App Store**: Handles payments and subscriptions
- **Apple CloudKit** (optional): Provides encrypted cloud storage for translation history
- **Analytics providers**: Anonymous crash reporting and performance monitoring

These providers have access to your information only to perform these tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

### 3.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- Audio recordings are stored temporarily in memory and then discarded
- Translation history is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database
- No voice data is stored permanently on your device unless you save favorites

### 4.2 Cloud Storage (if enabled)
- Translation history is encrypted with AES-256 before upload
- Cloud data is stored in regions compliant with GDPR and CCPA
- You can delete cloud data at any time from Settings

### 4.3 Encryption
- All network communications use TLS/SSL encryption
- Cloud data is encrypted at rest
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## 5. Data Retention

- **Audio recordings**: Deleted immediately after translation completes
- **Translation history**: Retained until you delete it or disable Cloud Sync
- **Analytics data**: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days
- **Account information**: Retained as long as your account is active

You may request deletion of all your data by contacting support@wooftalk.app.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data (translation history, account info)
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data (including cloud-stored history)
- Opt out of analytics collection in Settings
- Export your data in a portable format

### 6.2 Children's Privacy
Our app does not collect personal information from children under 13. We encourage parents to supervise children's use of the app.

### 6.3 GDPR (EU Residents)
If you are a resident of the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to be informed
- Right of access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@wooftalk.app

### 6.4 CCPA (California Residents)
California residents have rights under CCPA:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@wooftalk.app

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or advertising identifiers in the app. Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework is not used because we do not track users across apps or websites.

## 8. Third-Party Services

### 8.1 Apple Services
- **App Store**: Handles app distribution and in-app purchases
- **Apple Pay**: Payment processing (subscriptions)
- **CloudKit**: Optional cloud storage for user data
- **StoreKit**: Subscription management

Apple's privacy policies apply to these services.

### 8.2 Analytics
We use the following analytics services:
- **Apple's App Analytics**: For conversion tracking and App Store performance
- **Crashlytics** (if enabled): For crash reporting (opt-in)

These services collect anonymous data only.

## 9. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an email notification (for account holders)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes become effective immediately upon posting.
