WoofTalk translates between human and animal languages with voice input/output.

🐾 Core Features
• Translate human text to Dog, Cat, or Bird language
• Translate animal sounds back to human text
• Voice input with speech recognition
• Voice output with text-to-speech
• 100+ phrase vocabulary

🌐 Community
• Browse community-contributed phrases
• Submit your own translations
• Vote on phrases
• Follow other translators
• Leaderboards

🔄 Cross-Platform
• Sync your history across iOS and Android
• Real-time activity feed
• Share translations with friends

📱 Works Offline
• Translation engine works without internet
• Queue changes for sync when online
• Background voice processing

🎯 Multi-Language
• Dog language (woof, bark, arf)
• Cat language (meow, purr, hiss)
• Bird language (chirp, tweet, whistle)
