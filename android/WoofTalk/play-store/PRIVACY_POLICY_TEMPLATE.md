# WoofTalk Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 7, 2026

## Introduction

WoofTalk ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and email address (during account creation)
- Profile information (photos, dog details, location)
- User-generated content (posts, comments, messages)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Usage data (features used, time spent)
- Location data (with your consent)
- Log data and analytics

### Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect information:
- **Supabase**: Authentication and database services
- **RevenueCat**: Subscription and in-app purchase management
- **Firebase**: Analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications
- **Google Play Services**: App functionality

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide and maintain our App
- Create and manage your account
- Enable social features (posts, comments, messaging)
- Send push notifications (with your consent)
- Improve our App through analytics
- Process subscriptions and payments
- Comply with legal obligations

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:
- With other users (according to your privacy settings)
- With service providers (Supabase, RevenueCat, Firebase)
- When required by law
- To protect our rights and safety

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication via Supabase
- Regular security audits

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Object to processing
- Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@wooftalk.app.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
