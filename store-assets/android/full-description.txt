Talk to Your Pets — Instantly Translate Between Humans and Animals

WoofTalk is the ultimate pet communication app that enables natural, bidirectional translation between humans and their pets. Whether you want to understand what your dog is saying, talk to your cat, or communicate with birds, WoofTalk makes it possible through advanced AI translation technology.

Key Features:

• Bidirectional Translation — Human → Animal and Animal → Human for Dog, Cat, and Bird languages
• Voice Input/Output — Speak to translate, listen to results with natural-sounding pet voices
• Cross-Platform Sync — Start a translation on your Android phone, see it on your iOS device, continue on the web
• Community Phrases — Browse, contribute, and vote on user-submitted translations
• Social Features — Follow other pet lovers, view leaderboards, track your translation activity
• Offline Mode — Core translations work even without internet (limited functionality)
• Real-Time Updates — Community phrases and translations sync in under 1 second
• Wear OS Support — Translate right from your wrist!
