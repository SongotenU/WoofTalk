## Description

WoofTalk revolutionizes how you communicate with your pets. Using advanced AI translation technology, WoofTalk enables bidirectional communication between humans and animals — starting with dogs, cats, and birds.

**Key Features:**

• **Bidirectional Translation** — Translate from Human to Dog, Cat, or Bird, and back again
• **Voice Input & Output** — Speak naturally and hear translated responses
• **Cross-Platform Sync** — Start a translation on your Apple Watch, see it on your iPhone
• **Community Phrases** — Browse thousands of user-submitted translations
• **Social Features** — Follow other pet lovers, track activity, climb leaderboards
• **Real-Time Updates** — New phrases and translations sync instantly across all devices
• **Premium Subscriptions** — Monthly and yearly plans with 3-day free trial

Available on iOS, Android, Web, and Wear OS — all synced via a shared backend.
